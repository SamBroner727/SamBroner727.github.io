Biography

Sam hails from New York City (whats up!) and currently studies at Tufts University. Growing up Sam was not very cool, but luckily he's grown to love computer science!

Every summer, Sam works at a sports camp in the Berkshires called Camp Lenox. While there he leads a group of 40 7th grade boys. While at Lenox Sam gets a heavy dose of rest and relaxation and works on his jump shot.

In his spare time Sam drinks coffee, plays basketball, and hangs out with his friends.
